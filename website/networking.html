Networking

Founders talk to other founders. The hard part is just finding them.

Building anything is easier with people who get it. We run light-touch introductions and small gatherings for people working on companies, side projects, or interesting problems in and around Stratford-upon-Avon.

What we do

No pitches, no panels, no name badges. The format is deliberately informal — a coffee, a walk along the river, an evening in a back room of a pub. The point is that the next time you have a question, you know who to ask.

Introductions

If you're stuck on something specific — hiring, pricing, sales, a technical decision — we'll try to put you in touch with someone who has worked through it. Tell us what you need; we'll see who we know.

Meetups

Small, irregular gatherings for founders and builders in the West Midlands. We'll post details here once dates are set. Until then, the best way in is to email us and say hello.

Who it's for

Anyone running, starting, or seriously thinking about starting something. Solo founders, two-person teams, indie hackers, people freelancing their way toward a product. You don't need traction, funding, or a polished pitch.
